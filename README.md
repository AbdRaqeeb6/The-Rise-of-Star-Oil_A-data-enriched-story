# The Rise of Star Oil: A Data-Enriched Story 📊⛽

## Overview
This project presents a data-driven case study on **Star Oil**, 
an indigenous Ghanaian fuel retailer that successfully carved 
out a dominant market position against multinational giants 
such as Shell, TotalEnergies, and Puma.

Through strategic pricing, smart station placement, and 
unyielding quality compliance, Star Oil transformed from a 
fledgling local brand into one of Ghana's leading Oil 
Marketing Companies (OMCs).

## Key Highlights

- 🏆 **Market Leader** — Star Oil holds the largest share 
  among indigenous OMCs in Ghana (Jun 2025)
- 💰 **Low-Margin, High-Volume Pricing** — consistently 
  priced ₵0.20–₵0.40 cheaper per litre than major competitors
- 📍 **Strategic Station Placement** — positioned along major 
  highways, peri-urban communities, and high-volume transit areas
- ✅ **Quality Compliance** — consistently ranked by the NPA 
  as one of the most compliant fuel retailers in Ghana

## The Three Core Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|---|---|
| Low-Margin, High-Volume Pricing | Competitive pricing strategy that drives high fuel turnover |
| Strategic Station Placement | Locations optimized for maximum accessibility and convenience |
| Unyielding Quality Compliance | NPA-backed reputation for fuel reliability and zero adulteration |
